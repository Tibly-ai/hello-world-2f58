The Manifesto

WHY WE STILL
PRINT TWO WORDS

We started this shop in a garage with one hand-fed press and a stack of cotton paper. Every job that's come through since has followed the same rule the first program did: say the thing plainly and print it where it can be seen.

01

One Color Does Enough

Black ink, white stock, nothing dodging the point. If a design needs five colors to land, it wasn't finished.

02

Big Enough To Trip Over

Every poster leaves our press at a size that's hard to ignore on a wall. Timid type doesn't get hung up.

03

Made By Hand, Checked Twice

Every sheet still gets fed through by a person, not a batch job. Since 2019, we've never shipped a run we hadn't held first.
